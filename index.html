Our Mission

WDPP

FRHSD's Web Design Pathway Program is a 3–year program focused on preparing students for a future career in Web Design (or related field). The three courses that make up the program are:

  • Designed to teach students how to design and create functional and professional websites from scratch.
  • Built to help students explore college & career options related to the Web Design industry, complete the program with industry credentials, AND develop a passion for the creative field of Web Design.
  • FUN and filled with unique projects in which students can show off their own personalities, abilities, and interests!

EXPECTED OUTCOMES & POSSIBILITIES

  • Chance to develop marketable skills in a rapidly expanding field College readiness
  • Potential to receive industry credentials via NOCTI Test
  • Digital portfolio of student work
  • Weighted (honors) classes that benefit your GPA
  • Opportunity to show off your own unique personality, abilities, and interests through real-world projects
